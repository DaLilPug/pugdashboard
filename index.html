How it works

Three steps from inbox to sent.

No new tabs, no copy-paste, no rewriting from scratch every time. Reply Buddy lives inside LinkedIn.

1

Open any LinkedIn chat

The inbox, a profile, a chat bubble from the home feed - Reply Buddy notices the moment a thread opens and starts reading.

2

Get a reply, drafted for you

A "Blurb" appears in your sidebar within a second. Written in your tone, anchored on what they actually said. Edit or send as-is.

3

One click queues the follow-up

Hit the envelope icon when you send. Reply Buddy logs the message and lines up the next nudge so warm threads stay warm.
