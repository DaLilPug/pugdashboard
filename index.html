Quick answers

The stuff people ask before installing.

Will LinkedIn flag my account?
Reply Buddy reads what's on the page and drafts text into a sidebar you control. It doesn't auto-send messages, doesn't scrape profiles you haven't opened, and doesn't simulate clicks. The reply is yours to send (or not).
Can I edit Reply Buddy's drafts?
Always. Drafts appear in the sidebar, not in LinkedIn's reply box. You copy, paste into LinkedIn, edit freely, and send when it sounds right. The "Edit reply instructions" drawer in the sidebar lets you teach the AI your tone - more formal, more casual, specific sign-offs, anything - and saves it per account so future drafts pick it up.
What does it cost?
$15 per seat per month, or $120 per seat per year, with a 7-day free trial. One seat per LinkedIn account you're tracking. The Chrome extension and sign-up are free; Reply Buddy starts drafting replies once you add a LinkedIn account in the dashboard, which is what kicks off the trial. Each seat covers both Reply Buddy (the AI sidebar inside LinkedIn) and Tracker (the dashboard at app.uptown.com with analytics, cadences, follow-ups, and HubSpot/Zapier/Calendly/Apollo integrations).
Do I need Sales Navigator?
No. Reply Buddy works on every LinkedIn surface including the free tier, and Tracker's analytics + cadences + follow-up automation all run off whatever you log via Reply Buddy. Sales Navigator is purely about volume: its bulk-log bar lets you process a whole saved-search list in one motion instead of one prospect at a time. Worth it once you're past ~50 outreaches a week.
The dashboard side

Tracker is where you measure, follow up, and integrate.

Every seat includes the Tracker dashboard at app.uptown.com. It's where you set up your LinkedIn accounts (which is what activates Reply Buddy), see campaign analytics across every funnel stage, run follow-up cadences automatically, and wire Uptown into your other tools.

  • Per-LinkedIn-account analytics: acceptance, response, positive-response, and meeting rates - so you can see exactly which campaign converts and which one needs a rewrite
  • Bulk-log a whole Sales Navigator saved-search list in one motion - no clicking row by row
  • Follow-up cadences that run themselves: define the sequence once, Tracker queues each step on the right day for the right prospect (and skips weekends if you want)
  • HubSpot contact sync, Zapier triggers, Calendly auto-logging, and Apollo email enrichment - all wired in, no copy-paste between tools
