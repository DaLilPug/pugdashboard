Quick answers

The stuff people ask before installing.

Will LinkedIn flag my account?
Reply Buddy reads what's on the page and drafts text into a sidebar you control. It doesn't auto-send messages, doesn't scrape profiles you haven't opened, and doesn't simulate clicks. The reply is yours to send (or not).
Do you train AI on my messages?
No. Your threads are sent to Anthropic at draft time to generate a blurb, then discarded. We don't keep a corpus of your conversations and nothing you write is used to train models.
What does it cost?
The Chrome extension is free to install. Reply Buddy works for anyone signed in. Tracker (the dashboard, cadences, follow-up automation) is $15 per seat per month - one seat per LinkedIn account you're tracking. You can run Reply Buddy without ever opening the Tracker side.
Do I need Sales Navigator?
For Reply Buddy, no. It works on every LinkedIn surface, including the free tier. Sales Navigator only matters if you want to layer on Tracker workflows (bulk-logging, saved-search pipelines, cadence automation).
