1.Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: name, email address, organization, password, and role.
  • Outreach activity you log: connection requests, messages sent, follow-ups, prospect names, titles, companies, message bodies, response text, sentiment classifications, and meeting outcomes.
  • Prospect data: names, titles, companies, LinkedIn profile URLs, Sales Navigator URLs, and any contact information (email, phone) you choose to add.
  • Configuration: message templates, follow-up cadence, AI reply instructions, and account settings.
  • Communications with us: emails or support requests you send.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages viewed, features used, timestamps, and basic device information (browser, operating system).
  • Authentication tokens: issued by our authentication provider so you stay signed in across sessions.
  • Audit logs: records of administrative actions taken in your organization (invites, role changes, account creation, outreach edits/deletes) for security and compliance.

Information collected via the browser extension

The Uptown Outreach Tracker Chrome extension reads the contents of the LinkedIn pages you actively view in order to (a) extract prospect details from Sales Navigator search results when you choose to log them, (b) extract the contents of LinkedIn message threads you have open when you choose to log a response, and (c) draft AI suggested replies to messages you have read. The extension does not browse LinkedIn on your behalf, send messages on your behalf, or run in the background outside of LinkedIn pages.

2.How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service;
  • Authenticate users and protect against unauthorized access;
  • Generate the analytics, dashboards, and benchmarking insights that are the core of the Service;
  • Power AI features, such as suggested replies and outreach analysis (see §5 for the providers involved);
  • Send transactional communications (account, billing, security);
  • Send service updates and, where you opt in, product news;
  • Investigate and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service;
  • Comply with legal obligations;
  • In de-identified, aggregated form, to improve the Service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use Customer Data to train third-party generative AI models for purposes other than serving you.

3.Legal Bases (EEA / UK)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing your information are:

  • Performance of a contract (providing the Service you signed up for);
  • Legitimate interests (operating, securing, and improving the Service);
  • Consent (where required, e.g., marketing emails or non-essential cookies);
  • Legal obligation (when we must retain or disclose information to comply with law).

4.How We Share Information

  • Within your organization. Other operators in your organization may see Customer Data scoped to the accounts they have access to, and organization admins may see audit-log activity for the organization.
  • Subprocessors. We share information with the service providers listed in §5 to host, secure, and operate the Service.
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that it is required by law, court order, or other legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Uptown, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • With your consent. For any other purpose disclosed at the time you provide the information.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for advertising.

6.Data Retention

We retain Customer Data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service. After your account is closed:

  • Account information and Customer Data are deleted within ninety (90) days, except where we are required to retain them by law or where a backup retention window has not yet elapsed (typically up to thirty (30) additional days).
  • Audit logs may be retained longer for security, fraud-prevention, and legal-compliance purposes.
  • De-identified, aggregated data may be retained indefinitely.

You can request deletion of your account at any time by emailing austin@uptown.com.

7.Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS / TLS);
  • Encryption at rest for stored Customer Data;
  • Database row-level security so each operator can only access data within their organization and the accounts they have been granted;
  • Audit logging of administrative actions;
  • Access controls and authentication via our identity provider.

No system is perfectly secure. If you become aware of a security issue, please contact us at austin@uptown.com.

8.Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate information;
  • Delete your information (subject to legal exceptions);
  • Port your information in a machine-readable format;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email austin@uptown.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

9.Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar storage mechanisms (browser localStorage, Chrome extension storage) for essential purposes:

  • Authentication. Keeping you signed in.
  • Preferences. Remembering your selected account, sort order, filters, and similar UI state.

We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or analytics cookies that fingerprint individual users.

10.Children

The Service is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

11.International Transfers

Uptown is based in the United States and our subprocessors are listed in §5. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using the Service, you consent to that transfer. We rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) where required by law.

12.California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have the rights described in §8 above. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. For requests, contact austin@uptown.com. You may also designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf, subject to verification.

13.LinkedIn-Sourced Data

The browser extension extracts publicly visible information from LinkedIn pages that you, while signed in to your own LinkedIn account, choose to load and log. We do not bypass LinkedIn's access controls, and we do not collect data about LinkedIn users with whom you have not interacted through your normal LinkedIn use.

Uptown is not affiliated with LinkedIn Corporation. If you are a LinkedIn user whose information appears in a customer's Uptown account and you wish to be removed, please contact the customer directly. If you cannot reach them, we will assist where reasonable; email austin@uptown.com.

14.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide reasonable notice (for example, by email or via the dashboard). The updated policy takes effect on the date posted unless otherwise stated.
